RESOURCES & REFERENCES


Here are some resources and references about propositional logic and applications of boolean algebra with basic electric circuits:


1. PROPOSITIONAL LOGIC
ACADEMIC REFERENCES
* Discrete Mathematics and Its Applications, Rosen
* Logic in Computer Science; Huth, Ryan
* Introduction to Mathematical Logic, Mendelssohn
* Sets, Logic, and Axiomatic Theories, Freeman
* Introduction to Logic, Suppes


PRIMARY RESOURCES
* Organon, Aristotle
* Laws of Thought, George Boole
* Symbolic Logic, Lewis Carrol


2. BOOLEAN ALGEBRA
ACADEMIC REFERENCES
* Discrete Mathematics and Its Applications, Rosen
* Applied Boolean Algebra, Hohn
* Switching and Finite Automata Theory, Kohavi
* Discrete Mathematical Structures with Applications to Computer Science; Tremblay,Manohar
* Introduction to Digital Logic Design, Hayes
* Contemporary Logic Design; Katz, Borrielo

PRIMARY RESOURCES
* A Symbolic Analysis of Relay and Switching Circuits, Shannon
* Laws of Thought, George Boole
